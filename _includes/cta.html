Did Obama Break the Net?

Not quite yet.

We need your urgent help to pressure the White House.

President Obama still calls himself a strong supporter of Net Neutrality — the principle that cable companies like Comcast shouldn't be able to discriminate against some websites while making it easier to access others (like the ones they own, that pay them fees, or that they like for political reasons).

Yet the Wall Street Journal just reported that the Federal Communications Commission is preparing a new rule that would let Internet providers discriminate and charge fees — even though Obama appointed a majority of the FCC's members, including the chairman! (Who used to be a lobbyist for Big Cable, by the way…)
